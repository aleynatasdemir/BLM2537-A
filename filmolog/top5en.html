THE SHAWSHANK REDEMPTION (1994)

Over the course of several years, two prisoners form a friendship, seek solace, and ultimately strive for redemption through fundamental compassion.

Director: Frank Darabont

Cast: Tim Robbins, Morgan Freeman, Bob Gunton, William Sadler

THE DARK KNIGHT (2008)

When a threat known as the Joker wreaks havoc and brings chaos to the people of Gotham, Batman must face one of the greatest psychological and physical tests of his ability to combat injustice.

Director: Christopher Nolan

Cast: Christian Bale, Heath Ledger, Aaron Eckhart, Michael Caine

THE GODFATHER (1972)

Don Vito Corleone, the patriarch of a mafia family, decides to pass on his empire to his youngest son Michael. However, this decision, made unwillingly, puts the lives of his loved ones in great jeopardy.

Director: Francis Ford Coppola

Cast: Marlon Brando, Al Pacino, James Caan, Diane Keaton

THE GODFATHER 2 (1974)

The early life and career of Vito Corleone in 1920s New York City are depicted, while his son Michael tightens and expands his dominance over the family crime syndicate.

Director: Francis Ford Coppola

Cast: Al Pacino, Robert De Niro, Robert Duvall, Diane Keaton

FIGHT CLUB (1999)

A sleep-deprived office worker and a carefree soap manufacturer create an underground fight club that evolves into something much more.

Director: David Fincher

Cast: Brad Pitt, Edward Norton, Meat Loaf, Zach Grenier
